Recent Codex builds have been quietly hinting at GPT-5.6 before OpenAI's said a word publicly. The tell-tale signs: three model tiers codenamed Sol, Terra, and Luna, a new reasoning-effort slider replacing the old preset buttons, and a subagent-driven "ultra" mode for the gnarliest tasks.

Right now it's only reachable by vetted partners through Codex and the API, no ChatGPT access yet. Given how loud the Claude Code vs. Codex rivalry has been all year, don't be surprised if this drops right when it'll make the most noise.
