GitHub flipped Copilot over to usage-based billing on June 1, 2026, and the fallout has been loud. Where Copilot Pro used to be a flat $10/month (and Pro+ a flat $39/month) for effectively unlimited requests across models, you're now billed per token burned.

The numbers people are posting are rough. One developer says their bill is on track to jump from about $29 a month to nearly $750 for the same workload. Another burned 16% of their monthly Pro+ credit allotment in a single session just fixing a website bug, with what they described as mediocre results. Reddit and X threads are full of people threatening to route around Copilot entirely, either going straight to Anthropic/OpenAI APIs or through tools like OpenRouter.

If you're on Copilot, it's worth pulling up your usage dashboard now rather than getting surprised by next month's invoice.
