Codex Remote just reached general availability across all ChatGPT plans. The pitch: start or continue a coding task on your connected Mac or Windows machine, then review progress and approve actions straight from your phone, using one-to-one QR pairing between each device and host.

It's a small feature on paper, but it lands at an interesting moment. OpenAI says internal engineering usage of Codex is up roughly 27x since November 2025, with even bigger jumps in research (56x) and customer support (32x). Put those together and Codex starts looking less like "autocomplete with extra steps" and more like a control panel for an entire dev workflow, one you can steer from your pocket.

Worth trying if you've got a long-running task you don't want to babysit at your desk.
