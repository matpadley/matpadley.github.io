GitHub shipped "AI credit pools" for Copilot cost centers this week, letting admins cap exactly how many of the org's included AI credits any single team can burn through — calculated automatically from that team's licenses, no manual math required.

It's a pretty direct answer to all those usage-based billing horror stories that have been floating around since June's pricing switch. If your company runs multiple teams on one Copilot Enterprise contract, this is the feature that stops one overenthusiastic vibe-coder from torching the whole department's budget in a week.
