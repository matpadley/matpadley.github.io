GPT-5.6 landed on July 9 and Codex user numbers went vertical: 6 million by the 12th, 7 million a day later, 8 million by the weekend. Within hours of the 7-million mark, Anthropic extended Claude Code's 50% weekly limit boost through July 19. Nothing says "we're paying attention" like matching your rival's growth spurt with a usage-limit bump.
